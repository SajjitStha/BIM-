Animation

Animation is the looping of multiple images at a given frame rate which makes the illution of object inside the images move. It is a effective graphical representation which comes handy in different fields.Starting at 2 dimensional we have reached to the point of quality 3d model render.

There are a lot of platform to animate your scenes and characters. Eg:Adobe Animater,Krita , Flipaclip, Blender(3d animation),Autodeskmaya etc.
